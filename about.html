Meet Matt Grimison

Many companies don't fully recognize the value of strategic communication — especially small businesses and startups. And how much are they leaving on the table because of that? Spring Tide Strategies launched to change all that, providing complete strategic communication consultation and connecting companies with their customers.

Grimison is a seasoned strategic communication leader with 32 years of varied experience, including 22 years in the aerospace and defense industry. He has worked for the Aerospace Industries Association, The Boeing Company and L3Harris Technologies, preceded by a decade as a print reporter in Florida.

In 2004, Grimison earned a prestigious Congressional Fellowship with the American Political Science Association, working directly on policy in the House of Representatives. He is a brand storytelling trainer who helps companies paint their picture in vibrant, engaging ways.
